Hubands Energy

BRAND DESIGNER

THE PROJECT:

Hubands are an energy solutions company who have been servicing Northland for over 30 years. I was brought in to update the company's look and create a modern way to communicate their product range to new and existing clients.

The project was broken down into 3 stages, starting with designing a new look and feel, including a modern logo, new typeface, and colour palette which stands out from their competitors.

Once the logo was in place the next challenge was to create a way of communicating their large product range in a distinct and clear manner.

The third part of the project was to provide a new fleet design to be rolled out across multiple vehicles and supply the company with a clear set of brand guidelines to show how their new identity should be used for the best results.
